Finishing Touch Sealcoating Website

I worked with the owner of a Sealcoating company to create a website that would help him reach more customers and provide information about his services. The site includes a contact form, service descriptions, and a gallery of completed projects. The site includes SendGrid emailing service, which allows users to easily get in touch with the business.

After initial deployment, I continued to work with the owner to update the site with new content and improve its SEO. This included adding new service pages, optimizing images, and implementing best practices for search engine visibility.
