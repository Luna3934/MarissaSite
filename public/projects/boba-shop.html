Augmented Reality HEB Experience

This senior project merges real-world grocery shopping with augmented reality and image recognition. In a team, I focused on an AR experience. I created an interactive AR tutorial that guides users through the app's features. To implement this into our Flutter app, I found an AR plugin that integrates both ARKit and ARCore, allowing us to create a seamless experience for both iOS and Android users. The AR tutorial uses plane detection to place virtual objects in the user's environment and object collision to create an interactive experience.
