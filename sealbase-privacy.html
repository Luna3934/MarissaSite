SealBase Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 8, 2026

SealBase is a job management app built for sealcoating contractors. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can control it. We keep things simple because we only collect what we actually need.

1. Who We Are

SealBase is developed and operated as an independent application. If you have questions about this policy, you can reach us at sealbaseapp@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Account information — your email address and password (or Google account credentials if you sign in with Google)
  • Business profile information — your business name, logo, service rates, and contact details that you enter into the app
  • Job data — customer names, addresses, job notes, measurements, estimates, and proposals that you create within the app
  • Photos — job site photos you choose to upload
  • Location data — approximate location used for map-based area measurement features, only when you actively use the map tool

We do not collect payment information. We do not collect data from your customers directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve SealBase:

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To store and sync your jobs, estimates, and proposals across sessions
  • To generate PDF proposals using your business information
  • To enable map-based measurement features
  • To respond to support requests you send us

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

SealBase uses the following third-party services to operate. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database storage, and file storage. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps — map display and area measurement. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication method. Google Privacy Policy

These services may collect certain device or usage data as described in their own policies. We do not control that data.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, which is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure. We use Firebase Authentication to protect account access and Firestore security rules to ensure your data is only accessible by you.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data — including jobs, measurements, estimates, proposals, and uploaded photos — is permanently deleted from our systems. This cannot be undone.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • Access and edit — you can view and update your business profile and job data at any time within the app
  • Delete your account — you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen in the app
  • Revoke Google access — if you signed in with Google, you can revoke SealBase's access via your Google account settings

8. Children's Privacy

SealBase is intended for use by adult business owners and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at:
