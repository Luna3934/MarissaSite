Augmented Reality HEB Experience

Flutter ARKit & ARCore
← Back to Projects

For my senior project, I worked with the HEB Innovation Team to merge real-world grocery shopping with augmented reality and image recognition. While working with a team, I focused on an interactive AR tutorial that guides users through the app's features.

To implement this into our Flutter app, I took advantage of an AR plugin that integrates both ARKit and ARCore, allowing a seamless experience for both iOS and Android users. The AR tutorial uses plane detection to place virtual objects in the user's environment and object collision to create an interactive experience.

Features

  • Image recognition for pantry/fridge scanning
  • Augmented reality tutorial
  • Generated grocery lists
  • Recipe suggestions
  • In-app grocery checkout

Tools Used

  • Flutter & Dart
  • ARKit & ARCore
  • PostgreSQL
  • TensorFlow Lite
