I worked with the owner of a sealcoating company to create a website that would help him reach more customers and provide information about his services. The site includes a contact form, service descriptions, and a gallery of completed projects.

After initial deployment, I continued working with the owner to update the site with new content and improve its SEO — adding new service pages, optimizing images, and implementing best practices for search engine visibility.
