Delete Your SealBase Account

Request permanent removal of your account and all associated data

⚠ This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your SealBase account is deleted, all of your data is permanently removed. There is no way to recover it.

What data will be deleted

All of the following data associated with your SealBase account will be permanently deleted within 7 days of your request being confirmed.

  • Your account credentials (email address, password or Google Sign-In)
  • Your business profile (name, logo, service rates, and settings)
  • All jobs, contacts, measurements, estimates, and proposals
  • All uploaded job site photos and files

No data is retained after deletion. We do not keep backups of your personal information.

Delete instantly from within the app

If you still have access to your SealBase account, you can delete it immediately without contacting us:

  1. Open the SealBase app
  2. Go to Business Profile (tap your profile icon)
  3. Tap the delete icon in the top right corner
  4. Follow the on-screen confirmation steps

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately and permanently.
